Visit Carlisle

Welcome to the Great Border City located in the north of England, containing a history spanning centuries and is in fact the largest city in England by area.

Top three activities to do at Carlisle

Carlisle United

Nestled in the heart of Carlisle, Brunton Park is the home ground of The Blues who compete in Football League Two. It offers a lively and welcoming atmosphere for football fans of all ages. Whether you're a lifelong supporter or just looking to soak up the local culture, attending a match is a great way to experience the passion and pride of the community.

Explore Carlisle Castle

It is a must-see for history enthusiasts and casual visitors alike. Standing proudly for over 900 years, this iconic fortress offers a glimpse into Carlisle's turbulent past, having played a key role in defending the border during centuries of conflict. Explore its well-preserved walls, towers, and exhibitions showcasing battles and royal intrigue. With stunning views and rich heritage, it's a must-visit for anyone coming to Carlisle.

Take a walk in the Lake District

The Lake District, just a short drive from Carlisle, is a breathtaking natural wonder. Known for its stunning lakes, rugged mountains, and charming villages, it’s the perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts and those seeking peace. Whether hiking, boating, or simply taking in the views, the beauty of the Lake District makes it an unforgettable escape into nature.
